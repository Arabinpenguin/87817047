Safety first
Fully fenced? Wheelchair accessible? Sensory-friendly? Safety, cleanliness, and lighting ratings from real parents — not algorithms.
👶
Age filters
Filter by Infant, Toddler, Kids, or Tweens. Parks that don't match your child's age are automatically hidden.

💚 Our Story

Built for Portia.
Made for every family.

ParkPal started with a simple frustration. As a San Diego parent, I kept taking my daughter Portia to parks only to find them unsuitable — not fenced, not the right age, no shade, no coffee nearby.

No app existed that thought about parks the way parents do. So I built one — for Portia, and for every family that deserves better information before they pack up the car.

"Built for Portia. Made for every family."

— Mustafa Fermawi, Founder & San Diego parent
🌳

Starting in San Diego

Launching first in San Diego, then expanding to California, New York, Arizona, and beyond. Every park Google knows about — in every city.
